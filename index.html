Privacy Policy

Hoback Club Locker

Last Updated: January 5, 2026

Hoback Club Locker is a private ski equipment management application for Hoback Club members and guests.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address
  • Display name
  • User role (owner or guest)

Equipment Information

  • Photos of ski and pole equipment
  • Equipment specifications (brand, model, length, etc.)
  • Equipment ownership and rental information

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Authenticate users and manage access
  • Track and manage ski equipment inventory
  • Process equipment check-in/check-out requests
  • Display equipment photos for identification

Data Storage

All data is stored securely using Firebase services:

  • Firebase Authentication for user accounts
  • Firebase Firestore for equipment data
  • Firebase Storage for equipment photos

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data is only accessible to:

  • Hoback Club owners/administrators
  • The specific user account that created the data

Camera and Photo Access

The app requests access to your camera and photo library solely to:

  • Take photos of ski and pole equipment
  • Select existing photos of equipment
  • Display equipment photos within the app

Photos are only used for equipment identification and are stored in your private club account.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Secure authentication via Firebase
  • Encrypted data transmission
  • Role-based access controls

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Change your password at any time

Children's Privacy

This app is intended for use by Hoback Club members and guests only. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app and on this page.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please contact us.

Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) - for authentication, database, and storage

Firebase Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
